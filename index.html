Hello There!

I'm a PhD Research Fellow working on the application of AI in Renewable Energy systems. I was trained an Electrical Engineer. Enticed by AI and it's capabilities, I decided to focus on its application in renewable energy systems.

Currently, I am a research fellow in the collaborative project AIMWind (Analytics for asset Integrity Management of Windfarms) between the Centre for Artificial Intelligence Research (CAIR) and Mechatronics Group at UiA. My focus is on machine learning and analytics for windfarm life extension.

This website is under active development. At the moment, you can find my publications at the Google Scholar link provided above. You can also contact me directly through the e-mail listed at the university website.
